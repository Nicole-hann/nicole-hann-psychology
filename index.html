About Me

About Me & My Therapeutic Style

I have been in private practice for eight years, with earlier experience in counselling roles. I am dedicated to supporting individuals — primarily adults, and have a particular interest in working with those who have experienced trauma in earlier stages of life.

I prioritise creating a therapeutic space that feels safe, supportive, and person-centred. My approach integrates a range of evidence-based modalities, including attachment-based therapy, EMDR, Internal Family Systems, Schema Therapy, mindfulness, and compassion-focused therapy.

I place strong importance on building a genuine therapeutic relationship, as I believe this is the foundation for meaningful, in-depth work and lasting change.
