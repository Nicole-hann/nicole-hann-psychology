Services

Evidence-based, trauma-informed care

Specialised Treatment

Eye Movement Desensitisation Reprocessing (EMDR)

I am fully trained in EMDR and advanced EMDR, qualified to perform EMDR, which is currently one of the leading evidence-based treatments for adverse life events, PTSD, C-PTSD, childhood trauma, negative or stuck beliefs, OCD, Anxiety, Phobias and many other conditions.

For standard EMDR please allow a minimum of 10 sessions. This includes assessment, identifying targets/goals, increasing your resources, performing eye movements, installation and enough time to debrief and close EMDR smoothly.

To learn more about EMDR or to book an EMDR appointment, please visit www.perthemdr.com.au

Core Service

Individual Therapy & Assessment

I work primarily with adults, offering individual therapy and psychological assessment services. Sessions are available in-person at my Padbury practice or via secure Telehealth.

My approach is integrative and tailored to each individual, drawing from a range of evidence-based modalities:

  • Attachment-Based Therapy
  • Internal Family Systems (IFS)
  • Schema Therapy
  • Mindfulness
  • Compassion-Focused Therapy
  • EMDR
